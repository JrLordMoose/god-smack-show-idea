The Cast · 10

Key Characters

The boy.
The god. The witnesses.

Four faces carry season one. Each pulls a different audience — the underdog, the icon, the rival, the conscience.

PROTAGONIST · 01

Kairu

Commonborn · Clanless · Class unknown

No clan. No training. No explanation. Enters the tournament on pure instinct — and refuses to lose. The mystery of his true class drives Season One.

ANTAGONIST · 02

Vorath the Unfinished

Radiant Sovereign · Triple-class

His left arm is pure unsolidified light. Ancient. Patient. Allows the tournament because he finds challengers interesting.

RIVAL · 03

Sevryn Mythravax

Aurelian · 80 beads · Loresight / Veilwalker

Always masked. Always three steps ahead. He already knows what Kairu is — and is watching to see what Kairu does with it.

ALLY · 04

Omi Voudari

Kemari · Voudari · Wraithcaller

Speaks to the dead as casually as the living. Her space is always colder. Her presence unsettles everyone — except Kairu.

+ Brix, the Bound One · the hype-man. The animal mascot the merch team will love.

Pilot · 11

Episode One · 30 Minutes

The boy who
wasn't invited.

01
30 MIN·COLD OPEN → 4 ACTS → TAG

We open the series in the back streets of Centuria — not the marble plazas, not the temples. A clanless boy. A scavenged life. A tournament he is told he is not allowed to enter. And the cosmic accident that proves the gods wrong.

COLD OPEN
00:00 — 03:00
The Faded

An Ordained god of fire is challenged at the throne. He loses. We watch him erased from existence in front of his worshippers — universe-wide. Centuria celebrates. A child watches from a slum window. We don't yet know his name.

ACT I
03:00 — 10:00
Kairu

The cycle has reset. Halo Rings appear over every Commonborn god across Centuria. They do not appear over Kairu. He goes to the Hall of Acknowledgment. He is laughed out. Then assaulted. Then — for the first time in the show — something pushes back.

ACT II
10:00 — 18:00
Witness

Omi Voudari sees what happened from a balcony. She is the only one who recognizes the residue — the half-breath of a class no living god has carried in eight cycles. She finds Kairu before the Ascended do. She tells him to run.

ACT III
18:00 — 26:00
The First Bead

Kairu and Omi flee through a transit lane and land on an Unlit world. A village under siege. Kairu intervenes for reasons he can't articulate. Saves a child. The villagers pray. A Halo Ring ignites above him for the first time — and a single white bead burns into place.

TAG
26:00 — 30:00
The Watcher

Centuria. A masked figure receives the report of an unauthorized Halo Ring activation. He smiles for the first time the audience has seen. "Show me what you do with it, then." Cut to black. End credits roll over the sound of one bead clicking into a ring.

Franchise · 14

Franchise Strategy

The show is the cathedral.
Everything else is the city.

Eight expansion lanes, sequenced so each one funds the next and the IP scales without diluting the core.

PHASE 01 · CORE
The Anime

Season One. 12 episodes. The flagship that builds the world and the audience.

PHASE 01 · CORE
The Manga

Parallel manga release. Same canon. Different rhythm. Pre-launches the world before episode one airs.

PHASE 02 · DEPTH
Companion Bible

Illustrated world bible. Lore for the obsessives. Pre-order at announce. Becomes a bookstore object.

PHASE 02 · DEPTH
Collectibles

Bead replicas. Halo ring pendants. Clan sigil pins. Engineered into the show's plot — not bolted on.

PHASE 03 · SCALE
Mobile Game

Bead-walk gacha-strategy hybrid. Collect classes. Earn beads. Climb the tournament. Built for the existing fan economy.

PHASE 03 · SCALE
Console Title

Action-arena fighter. Devil May Cry meets For Honor. Each clan plays differently. Tournament mode parallels the show.

PHASE 04 · UNIVERSE
Live Events

Anime conventions. Clan meet-ups. Tournament cosplay arcs. Treat fans like worshippers — because that is exactly what the world says they are.

PHASE 04 · UNIVERSE
Spin-Off Series

Each clan can carry its own arc. Vordaan war drama. Anansi heist show. Sunari horror anthology. The world was built for it.

Phase 01 funds Phase 02. Phase 02 funds Phase 03. By Phase 04 the IP is paying for itself.
